Pancakes

To make batter you need the following:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  1. Combine and mix all the ingredients well in a large bowl.
  2. Spoon the batter onto the hot frying pan; cook on both sides until golden brown.
  3. Serve the pancakes with honey or jam.
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 200 g (≈ 1.5 cups)
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Egg 1 large
Milk 250 ml (≈ 1 cup)
Melted butter or vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Vanilla extract (optional) 1 teaspoon

Dumplings

To make dumplings, you need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 200 g minced meat (pork, chicken, or beef)
  • 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  1. Mix flour, water, and salt to make the dough. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine meat, cabbage, onion, soy sauce, and sesame oil for the filling.
  3. Roll out the dough into thin circles, place a spoonful of filling in the center, and fold to seal.
  4. Boil the dumplings in water until they float to the surface, or steam for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with soy sauce or dipping sauce of your choice.
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Warm water 1/2 cup
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Minced meat 200 g
Chopped cabbage 1/2 cup
Onion 1 small, finely chopped
Soy sauce 1 tablespoon
Sesame oil (optional) 1 teaspoon
